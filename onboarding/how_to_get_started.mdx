 Cube6 is a chatbot builder software that can automate conversations and streamline the lead-generation process. Our suite of features is well-suited to various applications across businesses, and we hope you find just what you are looking for.

## **Creating an account on Cube6**

Please follow the below steps to create your account on  Cube6

1. **Navigate to the cube6 Authentication Page**
   1. Open your web browser and go to cube6 authentication page

   ![3 Onboarding  Cube6 Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how- Cube6-works/onboarding/3.onboarding__ Cube6_resource_centre-1.png)
2. **Create an Account**
   1. Click on the "**Sign Up**" button to start the registration process.
3. **Fill in Your Details**
   1. Click on the "Work Email\*" field and enter your work email address.
   2. Click on the "Phone\*" field and enter your phone number.
   3. Choose a strong password for yourself.
   4. Click the "Sign Up" button to proceed.
4. **Verify Your Email Address**
   1. Login to your email account and locate the verification email from  Cube6.
   2. Open the email and click on the "Verify your email address" link to confirm your email and complete the sign-up process.

![3 Onboarding  Cube6 Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how- Cube6-works/onboarding/3.onboarding__ Cube6_resource_centre-2.png)

**Notes**

- Ensure you enter a valid work email and phone number to receive the verification email.
- If you do not receive the verification email, check your spam or junk folder.
