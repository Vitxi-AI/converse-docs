<Info>
  Note: The "Departments" feature is only accessible to users on the King Plan.
</Info>

In the **Departments** section, you can create and organize departments based on your needs. You can assign specific team members to a particular department.

## **How to create Department:**

1. Navigate to the **Departments** section.
2. \
   Enter a name for the department in the **Name** field.
3. Add a short description for the department, if needed.
4. \
   After filling in all the details click on the  "Add Department" button.
