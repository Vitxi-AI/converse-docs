### **How to train your AI Chatbot?**

To manage all your chatbots, you first need to visit the cube6 Dashboard:  https://app.cube6.com/home

To manage your bot, select it from the left sidebar.

![28 Other Sources cube6 Resource Centre 1 Pn](/images/how-cube6-works/other-sources/28.other_sources__cube6_resource_centre-1.png)

Click on **“Train Your AI Chat Bot”** and start training your AI Chat Bot.

![28 Other Sources cube6 Resource Centre 2 Pn](/images/how-cube6-works/other-sources/28.other_sources__cube6_resource_centre-2.png)

#### **Other Sources**

1. You can connect your Notion Page and your chatbot will retrieve information from it.
2. Similarly you can connect your Google Drive and your chatbot will retrieve information from it.

   ![28 Other Sources cube6 Resource Centre 3 Pn](/images/how-cube6-works/other-sources/28.other_sources__cube6_resource_centre-3.png)

Your AI-powered chatbot is now fully equipped to handle and answer all user queries seamlessly\!
